** Map Manager v1.1.0 **

**Features:**
- Show Player Arrow
- Center on player
- Reveal Map
- Survey Mapping Options:
	- Modify Range
	- Modify Surveying Time
	- Automatic use of Polaroid's (even if you don't have them)

**Not Compatible:**
- Map Tweaks (contains the some of the same features)
